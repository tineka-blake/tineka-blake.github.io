Hello, and welcome!

That's me, Tineka

A creator of dance and movement classes for the English speaking community here in Utrecht!

So far I have brought my love of dance to Sweden, Finland, Portugal, the UK, and the Netherlands, in the form of courses, classes and workshops!

Having been classically trained and certified as a dance teacher in the UK, my home country, I have been exposed to a wide range of styles from Ballet to the Bharatanatyam (of India), during my diverse studies and persistent travels. Not to mention also performing on the West End stages in London to Disneyland in Paris!

After relocating to Utrecht and settling into the international community, I was inspired to start teaching well-crafted and unique dance classes for adults that aids fitness and health, but also quenches our thirst of exploring different cultures and traditions!

I also offer private lessons and small group sessions upon consultation.

All of my classes allow you to learn confidently in the comfort of an English speaking environment.

Additionally, I teach pre-natal yoga, dance and barre classes here in Utrecht. I love to complement my yoga classes with the experience of guided meditation.

Yoga and meditation are tools that have been fundamental for my evolution on this journey so far! I look forward to finding more opportunities to combine these transformative techniques into a truly holistic experience!

Thank you for visiting. I look forward to hearing from you and seeing you soon!
