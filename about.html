About

Hello. I'm Tineka, a creator of dance and movement classes for the English speaking community here in Utrecht!

Up until now, I've been fortunate enough to be able bring my love of dance to Sweden, Finland, Portugal, the UK, and the Netherlands, in the form of courses, classes and workshops!

Having been classically trained and certified as a dance teacher in the UK, Â­my home country, I have been exposed to a wide range of styles from Ballet to the Bharatanatyam (of India), during my diverse studies and persistent travels!

After relocating to Utrecht and settling into the international community, I was inspired to start teaching well-crafted and unique dance classes for adults that aids fitness and health, but also quenches our thirst of exploring different cultures and traditions!.

Classes for children that combine dance, yoga, songs and games for heaps of fun! As a way to introduce children of the importance and marvels of nature through play!

I also decided to offer private lessons and small group sessions for those keen to focus on a specific aspect or area of dance or yoga.

All of my classes allow you and your child to learn confidently in the comfort of an English speaking environment. As I believe there is a world of difference between truly understanding the instruction of the movement and simply copying it (the latter can be fun at home!).

Lastly, I am also teaching pre-natal yoga, dance and barre classes here in Utrecht, where I love to complement my classes the experience with guided meditation.

Yoga and meditation are tools that have been fundamental for my evolution on this journey so far! I look forward to finding more opportunities to combine these transformative techniques into a truly holistic experience!
