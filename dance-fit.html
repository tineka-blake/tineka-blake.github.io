Dance Fit!

Immerse yourself in a range of cultures and traditions from all over the world, experiencing them first-handedly, through your arms, hips and feet!

This dance will have you feeling funky for Freestyle; sassed up for Commercial; seriously Street and sexy for Salsa! Be prepared to lose yourself in the beats of the music, oh, and not to mention, a raise in your heartrate, a tone of the thigh, and a loosening of the hips!

It's time to energise yourself, celebrate your body, and replenish your mind! It's time to express yourself whole-heartedly, for you to let yourself loose and to get into the groove! Sessions are without mirrors — there's no need worry about how it looks on the outside. It's all about how it good it feels from the inside and how much fun you're having!!

Questions?

Will the steps be simple enough for me?

Yes. No experience is required. The level of this class is aimed at beginners, or for those that have lost their connection with dance and want to rekindle the flame!

What dance styles or genres will I learn?

You will be introduced to a wide range of styles including, but not exclusive to:

- Afro-beats dance
- Street dance/Hip Hop
- Cuban Salsa
- Commercial dance (think Beyoncé!)
- Bhangra
- Modern Jazz

You will be in the hands of a teacher whose experience spans from Tap dance to the Tarantella, and whose music playlists spans from Mozart to Michael Jackson. (Don't worry, I won't be teaching you how to moonwalk!). Just expect to witness diversity, and a whole lot of fun!!

Will I learn choreography?

During the class, we will work with dance moves and short sequences that you can pick up easily in one class! Trust me. You will be taught in a fun, yet thorough manner, with steps and movements broken down into easily digestible pieces that we build up into short sequences! Additionally, we can revisit and build upon pieces to make a final piece de resistance!

I'm ready to have fun! Can I try the class out first?

Of course! You can opt for an introductory class for your first session at €12,50, before signing up for a flexible 10-class strip-card. The strip-card will be valid until the end of the year!
