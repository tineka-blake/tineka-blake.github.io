Dance Fit!

It's time to energise yourself, strengthen your body, and replenish your mind!

Now possible to subscribe as a virtual online class!

These energetic and motivating classes are crafted to give you a fun and joyful way to exercise that improves your overall fitness as well as your mental and physical health. With easy to follow movements, and guidance from a qualified and experienced teacher, you will be moving away to tunes and grooves that your body cannot resist! No previous experience of dance is necessary.
We have a supportive, social and non-judgemental atmosphere, without the use mirrors - so there's no need worry about how it looks on the outside - it's all about how it good it feels from the inside!
You are welcome to join our group of wonderful women, to socialise, dance and laugh with (laughter really is plentiful!), and watch yourself grow as your self-confidence and body awareness develops, along with more toned physique that really is a by-product of the whole experience!!

You will be amazed that such a fun activity such as dancing, has the potential to change your life. Gain control over your health and happiness and regain that mind/body connection!

Questions?

Will the steps be simple enough for me?

Yes. No experience is required. The level of this class is aimed at beginners, or for those that have lost their connection with dance and want to rekindle the flame!

What dance themes will we visit?

An example of the dance themes to visit are:

- Dancing through the decades: 1970, 1980's, 1990's ...
- Cuban salsa
- Commercial dance (think Beyoncé!)
- Dancehall & Soca (Caribbean dancing)
- Musicals (i.e. Hairspray, Chicago, Grease etc.)
- Modern Jazz

You will be in the hands of a teacher whose experience spans from Tap dance to the Tarantella, and whose music playlists spans from Mozart to Michael Jackson. (Don't worry, we won't be learning how to moonwalk!). Just expect to witness diversity, and a whole lot of fun!!

Will I learn choreography?

During the class, we will work with dance moves and short sequences that you can pick up easily in one class. Really! You will be taught in a fun, yet thorough manner, with steps and movements broken down into easily digestible pieces that we build up into short sequences! Additionally, we can revisit and build upon pieces to make a final piece de resistance!

What should I wear?

Anything you comfortable moving around in! Whether that's gymwear, t-shirt, leggings, sneakers... As you wish!

I'm ready to have fun! Can I try the class out first?

Of course! You can opt for an introductory class for your first session at €12,50, before signing up for a flexible strip-card.
