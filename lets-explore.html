Let's Explore!

Take the journey together with your little one as they find a love of movement through dance, yoga, songs, props and games!

Let your child explore their inner and outer world; flittering through the distant lands of the Amazonian rainforest as a beautiful ballerina butterfly or blossoming fruitfully from a small seedling to a sturdy tree. Together you will take part in their transformation!

Your child is discovering lots about the world through play, so each theme has been designed to allow their imagination and creativity run wild! Each Wednesday will be an opportunity for you and your child to grow together, have fun together and laugh together! Because what fun would it be without you there too?

At the beginning of each class there will be a 15-minute welcome session for you to arrive, have the possibility to chat with other parents in the community, and of course to have a cup of tea! A drink and fruit are always provided for you and your little one, which is included in the price per session.

Classes are inspired by nature, and are complemented by the environment at Nieuw Rotsoord, a wonderful location for toddler fun before and after the class begins!

Questions?

  • I have a baby to take care of, can I also bring them to the class?

    Yes, you are welcome! The class is aimed at 2-4 year olds, but if there is a baby sibling in the family, you are welcome to attend the class with your baby (free of charge).

  • My child is under the age of 2, can they still come along?

    Since development varies between each child, if you believe that your child will benefit from the class, but is under the age of 2, you can still come along for an introductory class. Just be sure to contact me beforehand.

  • Can my child join in at their own pace?

    Yes, the life of a toddler can sometimes be overwhelming, if your child has a moment where they simply don't want to join in or participate, that's okay. They can join in when they're good and ready!

  • Can my child experience a class before signing up?

    Yes! You can opt for an introductory class for your first session at €9,00 before signing up for a 10-class strip-card. (The strip-card is valid for 12 weeks, so you have a couple of lessons grace, if you need to skip the class for any reason!). If you choose to sign up after an introductory class, then you will pay for the remaining 9 lessons, providing there are still spaces to do so.
