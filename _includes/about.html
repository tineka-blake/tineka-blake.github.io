About

Dance is something so enjoyable that it's time to let go and dance like nobody's watching! My classes & workshops have been held in Sweden, Finland, Portugal, the UK (my home country), and the Netherlands.

As a certified dance teacher, I set the stage for you to let go, enjoy yourself and most importantly, to have a great laugh!!! In the end, isn't that what it's all about?! Yoga and Meditation are also my great loves which I teach weekly and are essential to my daily practice!
